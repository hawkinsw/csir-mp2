Nasal Polyp

I was diagonised with allergic rhinitis and nasal polyp in my right nostril last year and took steroid nasal sprays for almost 2 months and the main cause was the couple of cockatiels which I had. I gave them away to a friend and I got better. When looking into a mirror I can still see the nasal polyp, I want to know if anything should be done about it because last time my ENT did not say anything about the nasal polyp apart from prescribing nasal spray.

Hi, its unlikely that the cocktails have cause the polyp. Nasal polyps are polypoidal masses arising mainly from the mucous membranes of the nose and paranasal sinuses. They are overgrowth's of the mucosa. Nasal polyps may cause no symptoms, especially if they're small, and require no treatment. But larger nasal polyps can block normal drainage from the sinuses and they may also cause problems with breathing, when they need to be removed surgically. Good luck with your therapy. Please discuss this with your doctor am sure he will provide further assistance. Regards.
